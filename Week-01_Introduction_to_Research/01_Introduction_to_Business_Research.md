## 1.0 Objectives

After studying this unit, you will be able to:

* Explain the meaning of research
* Differentiate between science and knowledge
* Distinguish between inductive and deductive logic
* Discuss the need for research in business
* Classify research into different types
* Describe different methods of research
* List the difficulties faced in business research
* Explain the business research process and its role in decision making

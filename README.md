# Research Methodology & Statistical Analysis

This repository documents my **learning journey in research methodology and statistical analysis**, focused on building skills that are directly useful in **industry, analytics, and data-driven decision making**.

The goal of this repository is to develop a **strong foundation in research thinking and statistical reasoning**, with emphasis on how data is collected, analyzed, interpreted, and reported in real-world business and analytical contexts.

## ğŸ“Œ What This Repository Covers

This repository builds practical understanding of how research and statistics are applied in industry, including:

* Framing and structuring research problems
* Designing research approaches for business and analytical use cases
* Data collection and sampling strategies
* Measurement, scaling, and data preparation
* Processing and presenting data for analysis
* Descriptive statistics and statistical measures
* Understanding relationships using correlation and regression
* Time-based analysis and index numbers
* Probability concepts and probability distributions
* Estimation and confidence intervals
* Hypothesis testing and statistical decision making
* Selecting and applying appropriate statistical tests
* Interpreting statistical results for insights
* Writing clear, structured analytical and research reports
* Common research and statistical terminology used in practice

All material is organized **week-wise** to show a clear learning progression from fundamentals to applied analysis.

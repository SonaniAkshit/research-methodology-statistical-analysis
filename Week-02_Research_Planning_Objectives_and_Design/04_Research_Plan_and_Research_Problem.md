## 2.0 Objectives

After studying this unit, you will be able to:

- Select a research problem and identify its sources  
- Define a research problem clearly  
- Specify a research problem in a proper way  
- Explain why research objectives are important  
- Define a hypothesis and classify different types of hypotheses  
- State the criteria of a good hypothesis  
- Test a hypothesis  
- Describe what a research design is  
- List the main components of a research design  
- Distinguish between a pilot study and a pre-test
